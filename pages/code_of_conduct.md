Although not an ACM event, GCASR 2026 adopts the ACM SIGOPS code of conduct for conferences ([https://www.sigops.org/conferences/conduct/](https://www.sigops.org/conferences/conduct/)) with reference to the ACM Policy on Harassment and Discrimination ([https://www.acm.org/special-interest-groups/volunteer-resources/officers-manual/policy-against-discrimination-and-harassment](https://www.acm.org/special-interest-groups/volunteer-resources/officers-manual/policy-against-discrimination-and-harassment)).
