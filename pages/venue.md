## Louis Room

GCASR 2026 will be held at the lakeside **Norris University Center** on Northwestern University's Evanston campus.   
The address is **<u>1999 Campus Drive, Evanston, IL 60208</u>** — see [Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/KUxXeBAVg2BATYiK6) for planning your travel.

Parking in the North (**preferred**) or South (slightly longer walk) Parking Garages is usually **$9/day**.     

Northwestern can be easily accessed by the following public transit options:
- CTA (<span style="color: purple;">Purple Line</span>, Foster Street Station),
- Metra (UP-N Line, Davis Street/Evanston), 
- and buses.   

More detailed information about parking and visiting Northwestern by public transport is available at [https://www.northwestern.edu/admissions/visiting-campus/maps-parking.html](https://www.northwestern.edu/admissions/visiting-campus/maps-parking.html) and [https://www.northwestern.edu/transportation-parking/evanston-parking/permit-types/visitor.html](https://www.northwestern.edu/transportation-parking/evanston-parking/permit-types/visitor.html).
