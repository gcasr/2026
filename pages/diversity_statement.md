GCASR 2026 does not have a formal diversity plan. However, GCASR has historically made a strong effort to include participation from minority-serving institutions and to establish diversity within the leadership. Minority-serving institution participation from Illinois includes (from 2025) University of Illinois at Chicago and Northeastern Illinois University. 

The demographics of the six member organizing committee for 2026 are:
- 4 male / 2 female
- 4 white / 1 black / 1 Asian.
